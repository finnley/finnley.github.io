## 开启HTTPS

### 阿里云

可以在云服务厂商购买免费的ssl证书，然后下载
![](/img/wordpress/siteground/220.png)
![](/img/wordpress/siteground/230.png)

解压
![](/img/wordpress/siteground/240.png)


### 腾讯云

[免费 SSL 证书申请流程](https://cloud.tencent.com/document/product/400/6814)

申请免费 ssl 证书
![](/img/wordpress/siteground/320.png)

![](/img/wordpress/siteground/330.png)
![](/img/wordpress/siteground/340.png)
![](/img/wordpress/siteground/350.png)

打开站点ssl配置页面，选择【IMPORT】
![](/img/wordpress/siteground/250.png)

将证书 xxx.crt 内容复制粘贴到 【Certificate (CRT)】中

将证书 xxx.key 内容复制粘贴到 【Private Key (KEY)】中

最后点击【IMPORT】
![](/img/wordpress/siteground/260.png)

导入之后启用https，选择开发站点，点击右边三个小点，点击【Enforce HTTPS】
![](/img/wordpress/siteground/270.png)
![](/img/wordpress/siteground/280.png)

访问站点就会强制携带 https
![](/img/wordpress/siteground/290.png)
![](/img/wordpress/siteground/300.png)
![](/img/wordpress/siteground/310.png)
